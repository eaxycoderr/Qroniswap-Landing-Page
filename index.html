    Qroni Swap is a decentralized ecosystem offering crypto trading, NFT trading and staking, yield farming, creation and marketing of digital tickets, and many more exciting protocols in the DeFi and NFTfi space. Qroni Swap is powered by the $QNI token.

    Qroni Swap runs on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network. Its native cryptocurrency, $QNI, is a BEP-20 token.

    There can be only 2,000,000 Qroni Tokens (QNI) available.

    Qroni NFT ticketing allows event planners and organizers to create, sell, and re-sell digital tickets to their fans using blockchain technology and NFTfi systems.
